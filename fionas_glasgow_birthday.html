The Hidden Lane: Discover the quirky and colorful Hidden Lane, filled with artist studios, unique shops, and cozy cafes. It's a perfect spot to explore local creativity, pick up a special memento, or simply soak in the artistic atmosphere of one of Glasgow's best-kept secrets.

The Hunterian Museum and Art Gallery (Mackintosh House): Delve into the life and work of Charles Rennie Mackintosh with a visit to the Hunterian Museum and Art Gallery. Explore the meticulously recreated Mackintosh House, where you'll find original furniture and decor that showcase his unique design style.

Afternoon Tea Delicacies

Willow Tea Rooms: Conclude your day with a classic afternoon tea in a setting inspired by Charles Rennie Mackintosh. Enjoy freshly baked scones, delicate sandwiches, and sweet treats in an elegant, art-deco environment, perfect for a relaxing and refined end to your celebration.

Afternoon Tea Aboard The Red Bus Bistro: Experience afternoon tea with a unique twist aboard a vintage red double-decker bus. As you sip tea and enjoy cakes, you'll tour Glasgow's landmarks in style, combining sightseeing with the indulgence of a traditional British afternoon tea.

Mackintosh at the Willow: For a luxurious tea experience, visit the beautifully restored Mackintosh at the Willow. Indulge in a selection of fine teas, pastries, and finger sandwiches in this iconic tearoom, designed by Mackintosh himself, and revel in the elegance of a bygone era.
